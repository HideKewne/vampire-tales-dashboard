# Structural Criteria — Vampire Tales Dashboard (Variant 1)

## SECTION: Top Bar
1. Has "Vampire Tales" text on the left side? (yes/no)
2. "Vampire" in crimson red, "Tales" in gold/cream? (yes/no)
3. Has a search input field on the right side with placeholder "Chapter search..."? (yes/no)
4. Has a search icon (magnifying glass) in the search input? (yes/no)
5. Top bar has glass/semi-transparent dark background? (yes/no)

## SECTION: Sidebar
1. Has "Chapters" heading at the top? (yes/no)
2. Shows 7 chapter entries (Ch 1886 through Ch 1892)? (yes/no)
3. Each chapter entry shows chapter number and title? (yes/no)
4. The last entry "Ch 1892: Next Target" is highlighted/active with red accent? (yes/no)
5. Active chapter has a red bottom border or red highlight indicator? (yes/no)
6. Sidebar has glass card background? (yes/no)
7. Sidebar is on the left side, approximately 20-25% of screen width? (yes/no)

## SECTION: Reader Panel
1. Has chapter title "Chapter 1892: Next Target" in gold/amber color? (yes/no)
2. Chapter title is in a serif font? (yes/no)
3. Shows at least 4 paragraphs of chapter text? (yes/no)
4. One paragraph is highlighted with a distinct left border (gold/amber)? (yes/no)
5. The highlighted paragraph has a subtle background color different from other paragraphs? (yes/no)
6. Text is light colored (#F0F0F0 or similar) on dark background? (yes/no)
7. Reader panel takes up the remaining width right of the sidebar? (yes/no)

## SECTION: Audio Player
1. Has "My Vampire System" title text on the left? (yes/no)
2. Has "Chapter 1892 — Paragraph 3/40" subtitle text? (yes/no)
3. Has 3 centered playback buttons (previous, play/pause, next)? (yes/no)
4. The play/pause button is a large crimson red circle? (yes/no)
5. Previous and next buttons are crimson red arrow icons? (yes/no)
6. Has a progress bar below the controls with red fill? (yes/no)
7. Has "Speed" label with "1.0x" dropdown on the right? (yes/no)
8. Has "Volume" label with a slider on the far right? (yes/no)
9. Audio player has glass card background? (yes/no)

## SECTION: Chapter Navigation
1. Has "Previous Chapter" button on the left? (yes/no)
2. Has "Chapter Number (1892)" text in the center? (yes/no)
3. Has "Next Chapter" button on the right? (yes/no)
4. Buttons have glass/outlined style? (yes/no)
5. Navigation bar spans the full width at the bottom? (yes/no)
